‘Success is where preparation and opportunity meet’

In this era of escalating and never ending competition, there arises a growing need to stay ahead of the rest to avoid getting caught up in this rat race. Fueled by increasing speed of technology and the evolving world consequentially has led to the mushrooming of new fields and institutions, and thusa parallel rise in competitive exams. Unfortunately you end up applying for a few and miss out on the rest and have to go through the painless ordeal of waiting for year, owing to the negligence of a stressed out mind or simply due to lack of awareness and information about the various exams being conducted all around the country.

Go Alert India Private Ltd(goalert.in)an examination resource company was founded with an aim to fill this lack of awarenessby providing comprehensive examination related services, making sure you never miss out on an opportunity. We envision a platform where everyone has access to updated information about the numerous exams held over the year. We aim to create resources that can help aid students in keeping themselves up to date with the ongoing competitive exams globally.

Every year, there are over 400 competitive examinations being conducted across various disciplines. In such a scenario, it indeed presents a difficult scenario to stay informed about each and every one of them. Go Alert aspires to save the tiresome ordeal of constantly searching for the right exams that suit your needs, interests and skills by taking over the reins of responsibility on your behalf. We provide essential consolidated information a updates about the multifarious examinations in Engineering, Medicine, Management, Law, Civil Services and a plethora of other streams, only so you can concentrate and focus on your studies, without having to worry on missing out a single examination.

The pedagogy followed at Go Alert is to provide an individual-centric examination related propaganda focussedon your aims, needs and comforts. The list of examinations, tailor-made to suit you individually, and the updates corresponding to each one of them are constantly delivered as alerts or notifications through phone calls, text messages and e-mails.
